This is the first entry of this blog. I'm Eugene, a developer working on backends, tools, and the occasional small web app. I plan to use this space for short notes about software I build and ideas I want to think through in writing.

## Why a blog in 2026

Writing forces clarity. The act of putting a thought down — for a reader who is not me — drags the fuzzy edges into focus. That is the whole point.

## What you'll find here

- Build logs of small projects (the `/cc/` calculator is one of them)
- Notes on tools I rely on day-to-day
- Occasional opinion pieces

See you in the next one.
